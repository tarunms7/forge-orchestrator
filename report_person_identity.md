## Background

Paolo Maldini is a legendary Italian football (soccer) player who spent his entire professional career at A.C. Milan (1985–2009). Widely regarded as one of the greatest defenders in football history, he earned 126 caps for the Italian national team and won numerous titles including five UEFA Champions League trophies with Milan.
